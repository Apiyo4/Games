Ghana

Ghana was the first country in sub-Saharan Africa to gain independence post-colonialism.Lake Volta, in the Volta region of the country, is the world's largest man-made lake and has a population of 25.5 million. Major languages spoken by the Ghanese are English, Akan and Ewe. GHana has produced influential people like koffi Anan who served as the secretary-general of the United Nations from 1997 to 2006.

Ampe

Ampe is a simple but energy-driven game played by school-age children.

Game of Oware

The goal of oware is to capture the greatest amount of seeds as possible. To do so, players make moves in alternate turns until one of them has captured more than 24 seeds.

How to play Ampe

  1. The leader and another player jump up at the same time, clap, and thrust one foot forward when they jump up.
  2. If the leader and the other player have the same foot forward the leader wins a point.
  3. If they are different than the other player becomes "it" and plays against the remaining players. If the players are in a circle, the leader moves along the inside of the circle, playing against others in turn.
  4. If they are in a line, the leader moves on down the line.
  5. If only two players are playing, they keep score until a certain number of points determines a winner.
